40km/hと60km/hの平均
$$\frac{2}{\frac{1}{40}+\frac{1}{60}}km/h$$
$$\frac{2\times(40\times60)}{(\frac{1}{40}+\frac{1}{60})\times(40\times60)}km/h$$
$$\frac{2\times40\times60}{\frac{1}{40}\times(40\times60)+\frac{1}{60}\times(40\times60)}km/h$$
$$\frac{2\times2400}{\frac{40}{40}\times60+40\times\frac{60}{60}}km/h$$
$$\frac{4800}{1\times60+40\times1}km/h$$
$$\frac{4800}{60+40}km/h$$
$$\frac{4800}{100}km/h$$
$$48km/h$$
